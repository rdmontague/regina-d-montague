Personal Website Project Milestone 1



Purpose, Audience, and Goal


Purpose


This is my Personal Website Project, or PWP. Its purpose is to be a portfolio with a link to download my resume, as well as provide a contact form.



Audience


The intended audience is industry peers and potential employers.



Goal


The goal of this website is as follows:

  • To act as a portfolio of my best work
  • To land a job or paid internship
  • To make mom and dad proud
